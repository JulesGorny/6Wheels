Why do we like Mossy Earth ?

It is now commonly accepted that planting billions of trees to try to reduce the carbon dioxide contained in the atmosphere is one of the best solution with have to tackle climate change. But planting huge barren forest would do more harm than good. Everywhere in the world, natural habitats are destroyed, taking along all the animals that inhabits them.

Mossy Earth is not only planting trees, they recreate natural areas as they were before, allowing nature to regain its rights. And to us, giving back to the natural world what we’ve taken away from it is the best way to reduce humanity’s carbon footprint.

About Mossy Earth


Mossy Earth is a social enterprise on a mission to bring true wilderness back to Europe. Through the framework of rewilding and reforestation, we offer individuals, schools and businesses the possibility to plant forests and restore key ecosystems while mitigating their carbon footprint.
